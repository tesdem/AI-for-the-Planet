Glossary

Term Definition
Urban Heat Island (UHI) Urban areas significantly warmer than surrounding rural areas due to human activities
Albedo The proportion of solar radiation reflected by a surface (higher = more reflective = cooler)
Tree Canopy Coverage The percentage of ground area shaded by tree crowns
Green Space Ratio The proportion of vegetated area to total land area
Impervious Surface Surfaces that water cannot penetrate (concrete, asphalt, buildings)
Evapotranspiration Process by which water is transferred from land to atmosphere by evaporation and plant transpiration
